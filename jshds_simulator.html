🧮 JShDS qanday hisoblanadi?
JShDS yil boshidan o'sib boruvchi yakun bilan hisoblanadi (SK 388-m. 6-q.).

May oyi misoli:
1. Yanvar–may jami daromadidan JShDS hisoblang
2. Yanvar–aprel JShDS summasini ayirib tashlang
3. Natija = May oyi JShDS
